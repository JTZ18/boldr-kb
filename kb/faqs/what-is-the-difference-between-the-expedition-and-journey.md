The Expedition (40mm, Grade 5 Ti) is larger, heavier-duty, and rated to 100m water resistance — suited for active use. The Journey (38mm, Grade 2 Ti) is slimmer, lighter, and more dress-casual — rated to 50m. Both use automatic movements.
