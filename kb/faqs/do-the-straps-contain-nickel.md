The FKM rubber and nylon NATO straps are nickel-free. The metal buckle on leather straps is made from Grade 5 titanium, which is also nickel-free. The mesh bracelet uses 316L stainless steel — trace nickel content is present (as with all stainless steel). If you have a severe nickel allergy, we recommend the rubber or NATO strap options.
