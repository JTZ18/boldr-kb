The Expedition uses Grade 5 Titanium (Ti-6Al-4V), which is the aerospace-grade alloy — approximately 45% lighter than stainless steel and significantly more corrosion-resistant. The Journey uses Grade 2 (commercially pure titanium), which is slightly softer but still very durable.
