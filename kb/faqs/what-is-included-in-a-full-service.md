The Full Service (Standard, SGD 160) includes full movement disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, lubrication, regulation to +/-5 s/day, water resistance test, case light polish, and new gaskets. The Premium tier (SGD 220) adds deep case polish, crystal replacement if scratched, and a 12-month service warranty.
